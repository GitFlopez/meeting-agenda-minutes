# Meeting Agenda & Minutes Generator

**Stop running meetings that accomplish nothing.**

This ClawHub skill gives you 4 battle-tested prompts to run structured, documented, action-oriented meetings — from agenda to follow-up email — in minutes instead of hours.

---

## The Problem

- The average professional attends 62 meetings/month and considers 31 of them a waste of time
- $37 billion is lost annually in the US from unproductive meetings
- 63% of meetings have no pre-distributed agenda
- Most teams lose their action items within 24 hours of a meeting ending

This skill fixes all four problems.

---

## What You Get

### Prompt 1: Meeting Agenda Builder
Generate a structured, time-blocked agenda with owner assignments and outcome types before the meeting starts. No more "what are we even deciding today?"

### Prompt 2: Live Minutes Capture Template
A fill-in-as-you-go template that captures decisions, action items, parking lot items, risks, and dependencies — in real time. Works in Google Docs, Notion, or plain text.

### Prompt 3: Executive Briefing Doc
Transform raw meeting notes into a crisp 1-pager with BLUF, key decisions, sorted action items, and risks. Perfect for stakeholders who weren't in the room.

### Prompt 4: Follow-Up Email + Slack/Teams Summary Kit
Send a professional recap within 30 minutes of every meeting. Includes email, Slack/Teams message, individual owner DMs, and async thread-starter for Notion/Confluence.

## Quick Start

1. Run **Prompt 1** before your next meeting → share agenda with attendees 24 hours out
2. Print or paste **Prompt 2** template → take live notes during the meeting
3. Feed notes into **Prompt 3** → send exec brief to your manager/client within 1 hour
4. Use **Prompt 4** → send follow-up email + Slack recap before you close your laptop

**Time investment:** 15-20 minutes total per meeting. Every decision documented. Every action owned.
