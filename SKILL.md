## What This Skill Does

Turns chaotic meetings into structured, documented, actionable outcomes. Four prompts cover the full meeting lifecycle: before (agenda), during (capture framework), after (minutes + exec brief), and follow-through (email + Slack kit).

Most meetings waste 31 hours/month per employee ($37B lost annually in the US). This skill cuts that waste by forcing every meeting to have a clear purpose, owner, and action list before anyone dials in.

---

## Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Meeting Agenda Builder
**Input:** Meeting type, attendees, goal, time limit, any pre-reads or decisions needed  
**Output:**  
- Meeting title + type classification (decision / brainstorm / status / standup / retrospective)
- Pre-read list with links/owners (so people arrive prepared)
- Time-blocked agenda (topic → owner → minutes → outcome type: inform / discuss / decide)
- Ground rules (optional but configurable: camera on, phones off, timekeeper assigned)
- Pre-meeting checklist for the organizer

**Best for:** Project kickoffs, weekly syncs, executive reviews, retrospectives, all-hands

---

### Prompt 2 — Live Meeting Minutes Capture Template
**Input:** Meeting agenda (from Prompt 1 or paste your own), attendee list  
**Output:**  
- Structured notes template (printable / paste-into-doc format)
- Attendees / absent / guests fields
- Per-agenda-item blocks: Discussion Summary → Decision Made → Action Items (owner + due date)
- Parking Lot section (ideas / issues not resolved — logged for next meeting)
- Risks & Dependencies flagged during meeting
- Meeting effectiveness rating prompt (quick 1-5 for retrospective)

**Best for:** Any meeting that needs formal documentation — board calls, project reviews, client calls

---

### Prompt 3 — Executive Briefing Doc (1-Pager)
**Input:** Raw meeting notes or completed minutes template  
**Output:**  
- One-page executive summary (structured for leaders who weren't in the room)
- BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front) — decision or outcome in 2 sentences
- Key decisions made (bulleted, with rationale)
- Action items sorted by priority + owner + deadline
- Risks raised and mitigation owner
- Next meeting date / milestone to watch

**Best for:** Board reports, investor updates, escalation memos, skip-level summaries

---

### Prompt 4 — Follow-Up Email + Slack / Teams Summary Kit
**Input:** Meeting minutes or executive briefing  
**Output:**  
- Follow-up email (to all attendees): decisions recap + action list + next steps — professional, scannable
- Slack / Teams message (condensed, formatted with bold headers and emoji markers for at-a-glance reading)
- Action item reminder template (individual DMs for each owner — 48-hour check-in cadence)
- Meeting recap thread-starter for async teams (Notion / Confluence / Linear-ready format)

**Best for:** Remote teams, async-first organizations, client-facing meetings that need paper trail
